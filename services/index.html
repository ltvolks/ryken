Services

We provide a range of health care services:

Chiropractic care

Many patients are referred to our office with chronic conditions that have not responded to other treatments. Some have tried medication and physical therapy, and were left feeling like they just "have to live with the problem."

We offer patients a different approach so they may not have to live with their pain anymore.

Learn more about our chiropractic care.

Therapeutic Massage

We provide patients with therapeutic massage, trigger point therapy, and myofacial release to reduce muscle aches and pains, remove build up of adhesions or trigger points in the muscles, and decrease daily stress allowing the body to rest.

Relax, and have a moment of escape from your busy schedule.

Our professional licensed massage therapists are C.A. certified.

Learn more about our massage services.

Spinal Decompression

Dr. Hicks uses simple, yet sophisticated equipment which decompresses the spine in a precise, computer-controlled environment and is customized to meet each patients needs and physical attributes. It is manufactured by the leading rehabilitation equipment manufacturer in the US, is FDA cleared, and has thousands of installations worldwide.

Learn more about spinal decompression.

Sports Injury and Rehabilitation

We have advanced training in sports injury and rehabilitation techniques and we employ the latest physiological therapeutics (physiotherapy) along with state-of-the-art exercise and rehabilitation equipment.

Learn more about sports injury and rehabilitation.

Aqua Massage Tables

Aqua Thermassage is an unattended modality that provides relief from pain by using jets of warm water to massage the soft tissues of the body. The heat and massaging action of the water work together to help improve circulation, reduce muscle spasms, and increase circulation.

Thermamassage also helps to increase flexibility and joint mobility, has an analgesic effect, promotes tissue healing, and helps break down aberrant adhesions. It relaxes the nervous system and reduces stress, increases endorphin production, stimulates the lymphatic system to remove metabolic toxins, and soothes and relaxes.

X-ray

Should Dr. Ryan determine X-rays, MRI, or any other tests are necessary to better serve you in your chiropractic care you will be informed.

Additional tests such as these are on a case by case basis and will be determined by the doctor. X-rays are often necessary to visualize the location of any spinal problems, reveal any underlying pathologies, and make your chiropractic care more precise.

The Not-for-Profit Foundation for Chiropractic Progress has more information about x-rays and chiropractic care for patients.
