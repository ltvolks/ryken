Here's To Your Health!

The goal of Ryken Chiropractic & Wellness Center is to attain and maintain health at its highest level. My staff and I are personally dedicated to providing only the finest health care available today.

As a Doctor of Chiropractic, it is my obligation to serve, encourage, and empower others to achieve their goals towards better health. My objective is to stay focused on the latest techniques and to maintain a reputation of offering the very finest chiropractic and rehabilitative care.

Walk-Ins are Welcome!

We have a unique WALK-IN Policy. Once you're an established patient, and have had a few visits for your problem, any time you need a treatment, you can just COME ON IN during our regular hours.

If you want to call ahead, we can tell you if there is much of a wait. We know you're busy and can't always make a set appointment ahead of time.

In the beginning of healing, adhering to your treatment plan helps you get better faster. We are always willing to work with your schedule

Our Services

We provide a range of health care services, including:

Chiropractic care

Many patients are referred to our office with chronic conditions that have not responded to other treatments. Some have tried medication and physical therapy, and were left feeling like they just "have to live with the problem."

We offer patients a different approach so they may not have to live with their pain anymore.

Learn more about our chiropractic care.

Therapeutic Massage

We provide patients with therapeutic massage, trigger point therapy, and myofacial release to reduce muscle aches and pains, remove build up of adhesions or trigger points in the muscles, and decrease daily stress allowing the body to rest.

Relax, and have a moment of escape from your busy schedule.

Our professional licensed massage therapists are C.A. certified.

Learn more about our massage services.

Spinal Decompression

Dr. Hicks uses simple, yet sophisticated equipment which decompresses the spine in a precise, computer-controlled environment and is customized to meet each patients needs and physical attributes. It is manufactured by the leading rehabilitation equipment manufacturer in the US, is FDA cleared, and has thousands of installations worldwide.

Learn more about spinal decompression.

Sports Injury and Rehabilitation

We have advanced training in sports injury and rehabilitation techniques and we employ the latest physiological therapeutics (physiotherapy) along with state-of-the-art exercise and rehabilitation equipment.

Learn more about sports injury and rehabilitation.

Insurance

We participate with most insurance carriers and and our staff will be glad to review your benefits with you before your visit, free of charge. Many of our procedures are covered by insurance, and we bill your carrier.

Of course, we welcome patients who do not have insurance, and do our best to keep our fees as reasonable as possible so that no one is limited in the care they receive.

Auto and worker' compensation insurance pays up to 100% in most cases. Review our Insurance information for more details.
